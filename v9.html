Origin

It started with a question.

What if the chair wasn't just furniture — but an instrument? Josh Chaney spent four years engineering an answer: a personal audio environment that wraps around the listener with four speakers, vibroacoustic resonance, and a shell designed to disappear everything else.

Craft

Handcrafted in Chatsworth, California.

Every SoloDome is built to order. The fiberglass shell is hand-laid and sanded. The speaker array is tuned by ear. The upholstery is cut, stitched, and fitted by the same team that shapes the enclosure. No assembly line. No shortcuts.

Vision

Architecture of attention.

Sound is the only sense that moves through your entire body. The SoloDome honors that — it doesn't just play music, it builds a space where listening becomes physical, immersive, and deeply personal. A room above the noise.
