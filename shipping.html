Delivery + returns

Shipping & returns.

Last updated July 2026

Production timeline

Every SoloDome is handcrafted to order in Chatsworth, California. Standard production time is 4 to 6 weeks from the date of order confirmation. Custom exterior finishes or artwork may extend the timeline. You will receive email updates as your build progresses.

Domestic shipping

SoloDome ships to addresses within the contiguous United States via freight carrier. The chair arrives fully assembled on a protected pallet.

  • SoloDome Classic — $349 flat rate
  • SoloDome Deluxe — $469 flat rate
  • SoloDome Auditory — white-glove delivery included
  • Los Angeles area pickup — free, by appointment

Delivery typically takes 5 to 10 business days after production is complete. You will receive tracking information and a delivery window in advance.

International shipping

International delivery is available on request. Rates and timelines depend on destination. Import duties, customs fees and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer. Contact info@solodome.com for an international shipping quote.

Delivery inspection

Please inspect your SoloDome upon delivery. Note any visible damage on the carrier's delivery receipt and contact us within 48 hours at info@solodome.com. We will arrange a replacement or repair at no cost for shipping damage.

Returns

Returns may be initiated within 30 days of delivery. The product must be in original condition and packaging. To request a return, email info@solodome.com with your order number and reason for return.

  • A 15% restocking fee applies to returned items
  • Return shipping is the responsibility of the buyer
  • Custom-finished or artwork units are non-returnable once production has begun
  • Refunds are processed within 10 business days of receiving the returned product

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you would like a different model or configuration, please initiate a return and place a new order.

Damaged or defective items

If your SoloDome arrives damaged or defective, contact us immediately. We will arrange repair or replacement at no charge. See our warranty policy for coverage details.

Contact

For shipping or return questions, contact info@solodome.com or call 1.818.515.3130.
