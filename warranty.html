Warranty.

Last updated July 2026

Limited warranty

SoloDome Inc. warrants each new SoloDome product against defects in materials and workmanship for a period of one (1) year from the date of original delivery to the original purchaser. This warranty is non-transferable.

What is covered

  • Speaker drivers and subwoofers — manufacturing defects
  • Amplifier electronics and DSP components
  • Bluetooth module and connectivity hardware
  • Structural integrity of the shell and pedestal base
  • Power supply unit
  • Interior upholstery — seam separation or premature wear under normal use

What is not covered

  • Cosmetic damage including scratches, dents and stains from use
  • Damage caused by misuse, modification, unauthorized repair or accidents
  • Damage from exposure to extreme temperatures, moisture or direct sunlight
  • Normal wear of upholstery, foam and fabric over time
  • Damage caused during customer-arranged shipping or relocation
  • Products purchased from unauthorized resellers

How to request warranty service

To initiate a warranty claim, email info@solodome.com with your order number, a description of the issue and photographs if applicable. Our team will respond within two business days with next steps.

Depending on the nature of the issue, service may include:

  • Remote troubleshooting guidance
  • Replacement parts shipped to you with installation instructions
  • On-site repair for Southern California customers
  • Return to workshop for repair or replacement

Repair and replacement

SoloDome will repair or replace defective components at no charge during the warranty period. If a repair is not feasible, we may replace the unit at our discretion. Replacement parts may be new or refurbished and are warranted for the remainder of the original warranty period.

Shipping for warranty service

For warranty claims requiring the product to be returned to our workshop, SoloDome will cover inbound and outbound shipping costs within the contiguous United States. International warranty shipping costs are shared between the customer and SoloDome.

Out-of-warranty service

After the warranty period, repair services remain available at standard labor and parts rates. Replacement interior panels, speaker drivers and other components can be ordered directly. Contact info@solodome.com for a service estimate.

Contact

For warranty questions or service requests, contact info@solodome.com or call 1.818.515.3130.
