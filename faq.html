Product + experience

How can I try a SoloDome?+

Private experiences are available in Los Angeles, New York and Miami. A mobile showroom is also available to qualified customers in Southern California. Contact info@solodome.com to schedule.

Can the chair be customized?+

Yes. Exterior paint and interior upholstery can be selected with personalized guidance. Optional custom artwork and professional audio upgrades are also available.

What devices can connect?+

SoloDome connects to phones and smart TVs through Bluetooth and includes a 3.5mm input. It can also connect to computers, PlayStation, Xbox and other audio sources.

Delivery + dimensions

Will it fit through my door?+

The chairs are designed to pass through a typical 36-inch home door and can be angled through an opening approximately 34 inches wide.

How is SoloDome delivered?+

The chair arrives fully assembled by freight carrier on a protected pallet. Plug in the provided power supply and begin using it.

What does shipping cost?+

Domestic shipping is $349 for Classic and $469 for Deluxe. Los Angeles-area pickup is free. International shipping is available on request.

Ownership

What is the return policy?+

Returns may be initiated within 30 days of delivery. Shipping and restocking fees may apply. Contact info@solodome.com for assistance.

What warranty is included?+

Each chair includes a 1-year limited warranty. Repair support is also available through the SoloDome team.

Can the interior be replaced later?+

Yes. Replacement interior panels can be installed at home or by a local upholstery professional without moving the chair.

Technical

What is the speaker configuration?+

Two 8-inch full-range speakers and two 6-inch subwoofers create the binaural 2.1 architecture, with an overall 20–20,000Hz response.

What professional connections are offered?+

Balanced XLR inputs are available for $199.99, RCA inputs for $99 and a custom DSP curve for $399.

What safety and compliance standards apply?+

Critical components include a UL 62368-1 certified power supply, FCC-certified Bluetooth module and RoHS-compliant electronics. Upholstery and foam meet TB 117-2013 requirements.
